About Me

Hello peeps.

My name is Hélder Carneiro, and I’m a cybersecurity student who is passionate about exploring the world of cybersecurity.

One of my favorite things to do in the cybersecurity world is participating in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. These challenges allow me to put my skills to test and learn new techniques from other players. I also find CTFs to be a great way to build my problem-solving skills.

Through this blog, I want to share my experiences and insights on different cybersecurity-related topics. My hope is that my blog will help you understand and navigate the complexities of cybersecurity in a practical and engaging way.

In addition to my studies and participation in CTF competitions, I’m also involved in cybersecurity clubs. This has given me the opportunity to network with like-minded individuals and learn from cybersecurity professionals.

I’m excited to share my knowledge and experiences with you, and I hope that you’ll find my blog helpful and informative!
